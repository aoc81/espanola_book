Appendix — Reference Links Guide

This appendix is designed as a reader-facing source map rather than as a formal academic bibliography. It gathers the principal archived materials used in the project and points, where available, to the original publication URL. In the final book package, these URLs can be rendered as clickable hyperlinks.

Two cautions matter.

First, inclusion here does not mean that every source was treated as equally reliable. The book's method depends on comparison, attribution, and explicit handling of bias. Second, some materials were preserved as local archives, mirrors, or platform captures. Where no clean canonical URL was embedded in the saved file, the source is listed as a local archive or with the best recoverable public link.
