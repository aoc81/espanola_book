# Appendix — Reference Links

This appendix gathers the principal sources used in the project in a format designed for reader access rather than academic citation. Items are organized by category: core reporting and investigations; analytical, registry, and contextual sources; platform and channel sources; and local archive items.

Two cautions apply throughout.

First, inclusion here does not imply equal reliability. The book's method depends on comparison, explicit attribution, and the careful handling of sources with different interests and evidentiary standards. A source appearing in this list may appear in the book as a primary finding, as attributed reporting, as hostile-state commentary, or as propaganda being analyzed rather than accepted. Readers should follow the in-text attributions and chapter endnotes to understand how each source was used.

Second, some materials were preserved as local archives, platform captures, or mirrors before publication URLs could be confirmed. Where no clean canonical URL was available, the source is listed as a local archive. These items remain citable from the project archive and should be normalized to full URLs before any final print publication.
